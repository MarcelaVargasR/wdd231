Project Site Plan

Site Name

BOLD Trendy Clothes

I chose the name BOLD, to reflect a fashion brand that exudes confidence and contemporary style. "BOLD" signifies a daring and vibrant approach to fashion. "Trendy Clothes" emphasizes the brand's focus on the latest styles and fashion-forward pieces. This name effectively conveys the brand's identity and target audience.

Site Purpose

The purpose of BOLD Trendy Clothes is to provide a dynamic online platform where fashion-forward individuals can discover, explore, and purchase the latest in trendy apparel, the site will feature fashion advice, style guides, and trend reports to keep customers informed about the latest trends.

Scenarios

  • What are the latest fashion trends for the upcoming season, and how can I incorporate them into my work attire?
  • Are there affordable yet trendy outfits available that I can mix and match for different occasions?

Color Schema

The selected colors for the website are:

  • Eerie Black (#1F2421): Headings Primary headings (H1, H2) to create a strong and bold contrast, Navigation Bar Background color for the navigation bar to ensure easy readability, Footer Background: Providing a sophisticated and sleek look to the footer area.
  • Caribbean Current (#216869): Accents: Buttons, links, and other interactive elements to draw attention. Subheadings: Secondary headings (H3, H4) to add a distinct touch while maintaining readability. Icons: Used in icons and small graphical elements for a cohesive design.
  • Jungle Green (#49A078): Background Color: Sections such as banners or highlighted areas to create a fresh and inviting atmosphere. Call-to-Action Buttons: Ensuring they stand out and encourage user interaction.
  • Cambridge Blue (#9CC5A1): Background Color: For content sections, providing a soft and calm visual experience. Secondary Accents: Borders, dividers, and underlines to complement other elements without overpowering them.
  • Platinum (#DCE1DE): Background Color: Main background color to ensure a clean and neutral canvas for other elements. Paragraph Text: To ensure readability and a balanced contrast against darker background sections. Forms and Input Fields: Background for form fields to maintain a clean and user-friendly interface.

Typography

The fonts selected for the website are:

  • Roboto: Used for body text to ensure readability and modernity.
  • Merriweather: Used for headings to add a touch of elegance and readability.
